Our Team

When YBDS was started; it was a team of people who came together from different fields and walks of life to make a discernable change to society with the work that they would do using education as the primary tool...

Currently, the YBDS team comprises of people from different walks of life who have all come together with their varied experiences to work towards this one mission. The team includes people from diverse backgrounds including social workers, activists,corporate professionals, consultants, academicians, medical professionals, etc.
