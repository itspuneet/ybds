G)To enable YBDS to meet its expenditure on attaining and in implementing the aforesaid objects in proposes to generate funds and financial resources through the following methods:

3) To accept donations, grants, presents, gifts in the shape of movable and immovable properties for the achievement of the aims and objects of YBDS.
